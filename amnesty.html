Amnesty International

This project challenged us to get Millennials to take injustice personally and care about issues that may be far from home but that are affecting humans elsewhere.

Along with a partner, we decided to focus on homelessness, an issue that has affected so many Americans.

At the same time, Millennials tend to be very concerned with helping stray dogs and cats, and will often overlook another human being in need. This campaign aims to change that.
