DigiDoctor

The brief was to get Millennials to care about their health. Our insight was that that target prefer to stay online, in their comfort zone, instead of choosing something reliable to take care of their health. To fix that we created DigiDoctor, a platform where users can remain online but be connected with real life physicians who they will feel comfortable with, as well as reminding them of blood work and nutrition goals they set to keep them going strong!
