TOMS

The insight for this campaign was that Millennials need to be engaged in discovering their actions are, in fact, changing the world.

Since they are still interested in fashion but want to purchase products that will also do good in the world, the strategy was then to get fashion bloggers involved in the TOMS "Buy One Give One" campaign. With that, selected bloggers can design their own items based on the country they will be helping and those items bought will be able to be tracked through the serial number on a microsite.
